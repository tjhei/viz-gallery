Snapshot of a global convection model

by Rene Gassmöller and Juliane Dannberg (University of Florida).

The global mantle convection simulation covers 1 billions years of plate motion history that is prescribed at the surface and the graphic shown is the final state. Plumes are shown in red-yellow (using an isosurface of high temperature) and subducted slabs are shown in blue (using an isosurface of low temperature).

The simulation is done using the ASPECT code available at aspect.geoydnamics.org
